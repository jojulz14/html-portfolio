The best movies according to Mark

My top 3 movies of all time

John Wick

a 2014 action film about a retired hitman who returns to the criminal underworld to seek revenge after a gangster's
son kills his dog—his last gift from his late wife. His quest triggers a violent war with the Russian mafia.

The Pursuit of Happyness

is a 2006 film based on a true story. It follows Chris Gardner, a struggling salesman who becomes homeless while
raising his young son. Despite hardships, he never gives up and eventually earns a life-changing job as a
stockbroker through determination and hard work.

Catch Me If You Can

a 2002 film based on the true story of Frank Abagnale Jr., a young con artist who successfully posed as a pilot,
doctor, and lawyer while forging millions in checks—all before age 21. FBI agent Carl Hanratty pursues him
across the country in a game of cat and mouse.
